ABOUT MARK

After receiving my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from The University of Texas, I followed the family business and went into education. I spent over 4 years in Central Texas teaching mathematics to high school students. I also coached several school activities, including basketball and golf.

I studied programming and data science during the fall of 2016 while attending the Galvanize Data Science Program. Since Galvanize, I have taken numerous online courses practicing and expanding current knowledge and skills. Some of the courses include Deep Learning Specialization offered by Coursera and Andrew Ng, as well as the Data Science Specialization offered by Coursera and John Hopkins University.

Most recently, I have had the privilege working for several contracts for small companies, including an Internet of Things project designing software using a Raspberry Pi.

If you'd like to get in touch, or need would like to discuss a project, you can email me at meutband@gmail.com or follow me on social media listed below.
